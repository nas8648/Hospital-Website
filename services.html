Our Services

Medic Hospital offers comprehensive healthcare services designed to meet the needs of every patient, supported by advanced technology, experienced professionals, and compassionate care.

Ambulance Service

Our 24/7 ambulance service ensures that patients receive immediate transportation and emergency care whenever required. Equipped with modern life-saving equipment and staffed by trained paramedics, our ambulances can handle critical situations effectively. Whether it is a trauma, heart attack, or accident emergency, our team responds swiftly to provide first-aid and stabilize patients en route to the hospital. We maintain a wide network to ensure timely access to emergency care, even in remote areas.

Pharmacy

Our fully equipped in-house pharmacy operates round the clock to provide patients and visitors with authentic and quality medicines. We maintain an extensive inventory covering emergency drugs, surgical supplies, and over-the-counter products. Expert pharmacists are available to provide guidance, dosage instructions, and support for patient medication management. Our commitment to safety and accuracy ensures that every prescription is dispensed with the utmost care.

Laboratory Services

Medic Hospital offers state-of-the-art diagnostic laboratory services with accurate and timely results. Our laboratory is equipped with advanced testing equipment to conduct blood tests, pathology, microbiology, and biochemistry investigations. We ensure minimal waiting times and provide digital reports for easy access. Our lab technicians and pathologists follow strict quality protocols to maintain precision, helping doctors make informed treatment decisions efficiently.

Blood Bank

Our certified blood bank ensures the safe and reliable availability of blood and its components for surgeries, emergencies, and critical treatments. We follow strict screening and storage procedures to guarantee safety for recipients. Regular blood donation camps are organized to maintain an adequate supply. With 24/7 service availability, we are prepared to support life-saving procedures and emergency requirements efficiently.

Stroke Center

Our dedicated Stroke Center specializes in the quick diagnosis and treatment of stroke cases to prevent long-term complications. Equipped with advanced neuroimaging facilities, we ensure rapid assessment and emergency interventions like thrombolysis and rehabilitation therapies. Rural Health Center

Medic Hospital extends its healthcare services to rural areas through our dedicated Rural Health Centers. We focus on providing accessible and affordable medical care for people living in underserved communities. Our mobile units and outreach programs offer preventive health checkups, vaccination drives, and primary treatments. This initiative bridges the healthcare gap, ensuring quality medical support reaches every corner.
