Our Specialities

Medic Hospital is proud to offer a wide range of medical specialities, combining advanced technology, expert doctors, and compassionate care to meet the diverse healthcare needs of every patient.

Anesthesiology

Our Anesthesiology department provides complete anesthesia and pain management solutions for all types of surgeries and medical procedures. We use advanced monitoring systems to track vital signs and ensure comfort before, during, and after the surgery. Our team also specializes in critical care anesthesia, emergency pain relief, and post-operative recovery management. Patient safety and painless treatment are our highest priorities.

General Surgery

The General Surgery department offers surgical solutions for a wide range of conditions including gastrointestinal disorders, hernias, gallbladder issues, and trauma injuries. Our surgeons are experienced in performing both open and minimally invasive laparoscopic surgeries for faster recovery and reduced hospital stays. We maintain strict hygiene and use modern equipment to ensure patient safety and accurate outcomes. The department also provides post-surgical rehabilitation and regular follow-ups to monitor complete recovery. Compassionate care and precision are the core values of our surgical team.

Neurosurgery

Our Neurosurgery department is dedicated to treating disorders related to the brain, spine, and nervous system. We specialize in handling complex conditions like brain tumors, spine deformities, and traumatic brain injuries using advanced imaging and minimally invasive procedures. Our team focuses on early diagnosis, timely intervention, and post-surgery rehabilitation for better neurological health. State-of-the-art technology and highly trained specialists ensure maximum precision and safety during surgeries. Patient counseling and long-term neurological care are also key aspects of our service.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department provides complete heart care, including preventive checkups, diagnosis, and treatment for all cardiovascular conditions. We manage heart attacks, arrhythmias, valve diseases, and heart failure with modern diagnostic tools like ECG, echocardiography, and angiography. Our interventional cardiologists perform life-saving procedures such as angioplasty and stenting. We focus on early detection, lifestyle counseling, and personalized care plans for long-term heart health. Patients receive 24/7 emergency cardiac support for critical conditions.

Ophthalmology

The Ophthalmology department provides advanced eye care services for all age groups. We specialize in treating cataracts, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, and other vision-related conditions. Our doctors perform precise surgeries with modern equipment to restore and maintain eye health. Routine eye checkups and vision correction are also available to prevent long-term complications. We are committed to preserving your sight with compassionate care and the latest technology.

Dentistry

Our Dental department offers a full range of services including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. From teeth cleaning and fillings to dental implants, braces, and root canal therapy, our team ensures safe and painless treatments. We also provide advanced cosmetic procedures like veneers and teeth whitening for a confident smile. Our goal is to maintain long-term oral health with regular follow-ups and patient education. A clean, comfortable environment ensures a stress-free experience.

Dermatology

Our Dermatology department treats all skin, hair, and nail disorders, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, and pigmentation issues. We also provide aesthetic treatments like chemical peels, laser therapy, and skin rejuvenation for enhanced appearance. Our dermatologists focus on accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and preventive skin care. We aim to boost patient confidence while maintaining healthy and radiant skin with expert guidance.

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat)

The ENT department delivers expert care for conditions affecting the ear, nose, throat, and related structures. We treat sinusitis, tonsillitis, hearing loss, nasal blockages, and voice disorders with advanced diagnostic and surgical techniques. Our specialists perform endoscopic sinus surgeries, ear surgeries, and allergy treatments with precision. We provide preventive care and patient education to avoid recurring ENT issues and ensure long-term health.
